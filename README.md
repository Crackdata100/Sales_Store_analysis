# Sales_Store_analysis

## Store Background:-
A retail store chain track daily transactions, including order details, customers info, product categories, order times, and order status. The business wants to optimize operations,
improve customers experience and increasing profitablity using data-driven desicions.

## Problem Statements:-
The store does'nt have a clear about :
1. Which products sell the most,
2. Customers preference,
3. Which items bring in the most profit, and
4. Where things are going wrong in deivery or operations.
Because of this , they are missing chances to earn more, losing customers, and making poor business decisions.

## Solution:-
They need proper report and simple insights to understand their sales, customers, and product performance better.
